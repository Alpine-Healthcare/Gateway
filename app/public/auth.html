How does access work on Alpine?

Register using a Passkey (from your phone, computer, password manager, etc.) which creates a secure keypair owned and managed by you. Passkeys are the most secured method for encryting and protecting user data. The public key will encrypt all of your information and can only ever be decrypted by those who are permissioned to access.

When you start a session you download your Access Key Package, which is a group of keys you use to interact with the network and which determines what data you have access too. These keys are only ever decrypted locally on your machine.
